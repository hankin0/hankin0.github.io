I am a PhD candidate at Tufts University in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. I work in the Tufts Computer Architecture Lab (TCAL) under the advisement of Dr. Mark Hempstead.

My research interests are centered around building modeling and simulation tools to advance the field of computer architecture. I primarily focus on next-generation memory systems--those that include emerging nonvolatile memories (NVMs). I have also worked on building tools to study thermal hotspots.
